About

This is a text-based game in the messenger telegram. You begin as an inexperienced racer who has chosen a team. You can participate and win races, buy new cars, compare your successes with the successes of other racers, and invest in your team, so you will receive some bonuses. Also in the game there is a referral system. You can invite a friend into the game and, how to get some big amount of money, and add starting money to your friend. Good luck!
